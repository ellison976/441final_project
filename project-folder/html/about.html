About BHP Group Limited

Our Story

BHP is one of the world's largest mining and resources companies, with operations spanning across Australia, the Americas, Asia, and Europe. Founded in 1885, we have grown to become a global leader in the production of iron ore, coal, copper, nickel, and other essential commodities that power the world economy.

Our mission is to bring people and resources together to build a better world. We are committed to operating safely, sustainably, and responsibly, while delivering long-term value to our shareholders, customers, employees, and the communities where we operate.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To discover, develop, and deliver natural resources that are essential to global progress, in a way that is safe, sustainable, and responsible.

Vision: To be the world's most valued and trusted resources company, leading the transition to a low-carbon future.

Our Core Values

Care for People

We prioritize the safety and wellbeing of our employees and communities above all else.

Integrity

We act with honesty, transparency, and accountability in everything we do.

Innovation

We embrace new technologies and ideas to drive continuous improvement.

Sustainability

We are committed to protecting the environment and creating a sustainable future.
